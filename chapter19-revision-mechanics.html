Chapter 19: Revision Mechanics

📙 SS3 Second Term ⏱️ Reading: 45 mins

19.1 Motion (SUVAT Equations)

v = u + at  |  s = ut + ½at²  |  v² = u² + 2as
📐 Example: Car starts from rest, accelerates at 2 m/s² for 10 s. Find distance.
s = 0 + ½(2)(100) = 100 m

19.2 Forces and Newton's Laws

F = ma  |  W = mg  |  F = μN (friction)
📐 Example: 5 kg object, 20 N force. Find acceleration.
a = 20/5 = 4 m/s²

19.3 Work, Energy, and Power

W = Fd  |  KE = ½mv²  |  PE = mgh  |  P = W/t
📐 Example: 2 kg ball at 10 m height. Find PE.
PE = 2 × 9.8 × 10 = 196 J

19.4 Momentum and Impulse

p = mv  |  J = Ft = Δp  |  m₁u₁ + m₂u₂ = m₁v₁ + m₂v₂

19.5 Scalars and Vectors

ScalarsVectors

19.6 Density and Pressure

ρ = m/V  |  P = F/A  |  P = ρgh (liquids)

19.7 Simple Machines

MA = Load/Effort  |  VR = Effort distance/Load distance  |  η = (MA/VR)×100%

19.8 WAEC/NECO Practice Questions

Q1: A car accelerates uniformly from 10 m/s to 30 m/s in 5 seconds. Calculate: (a) acceleration, (b) distance covered.
Answer: (a) a = (30-10)/5 = 4 m/s² (b) s = (10×5) + ½(4)(25) = 50+50 = 100 m
Q2: A force of 100 N acts on a body of mass 25 kg. Find the acceleration produced.
Answer: a = F/m = 100/25 = 4 m/s²
Q3: An object of mass 5 kg is raised through a height of 8 m. Calculate the work done. (g = 10 m/s²)
Answer: W = mgh = 5 × 10 × 8 = 400 J

📝 Ultimate Mechanics Formula Sheet

  • Motion: v=u+at, s=ut+½at², v²=u²+2as
  • Force: F=ma, W=mg, F=μN
  • Energy: KE=½mv², PE=mgh, W=Fd
  • Power: P=W/t=Fv
  • Momentum: p=mv, J=Ft=Δp
  • Pressure: P=F/A, P=ρgh
  • Machines: MA=L/E, VR=ED/LD, η=MA/VR×100%
